Chapter 2 Summary

Key concepts and definitions from Sensation & Perception

Sensation Basics

  • Sensation Detection of stimuli
  • Perception Interpretation of stimuli
  • Transduction Energy conversion
  • Absolute Threshold Minimum detectable stimulus
  • Difference Threshold Smallest detectable difference

Senses

  • Vision Eye detects light
  • Audition Hearing sense
  • Olfaction Sense of smell
  • Gustation Sense of taste
  • Vestibular Balance sense

Perception Concepts

  • Gestalt Psychology Whole experience
  • Figure-Ground Object vs background
  • Perceptual Constancy Stable perception
  • Depth Perception Judging distance
  • Illusion Misinterpretation of stimuli
